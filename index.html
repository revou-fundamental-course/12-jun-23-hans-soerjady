Temperature Converter

This is a temperature converter. You can put your number on the first box below, and click "Convert" to convert your number to the desired unit, or click "Reset to empty all the boxes, or you can click "Reverse" to switch the unit conversions.



The formula for converting Celcius (°C) to Fahrenheit (°F)

[A] °C ≘ ([A] × 9/5 + 32) °F

or

[A] °C ≘ ([A] × 1.8 + 32) °F
